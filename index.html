Meet my Japanese Idols & Rock Bands [Galleries]

FRUIT ZIPPER

is a 7-member Japanese girl group formed in February 2022 under Kawaii Lab, a project by ASOBISYSTEM aiming to introduce Japan's idol culture to the world. The group consists of Amane Tsukiashi, Karen Matsumoto, Mana Manaka, Yui Sakurai, Noel Hayase, Luna Nakagawa, and Suzuka Chinzei.

THE PEGGIES

A dynamic Japanese pop group noted for their catchy tunes and sympathetic lyrics. With a sound that combines pop-punk intensity with sincere emotion, they represent the enthusiasm of youth as well as the ups and downs of love and friendship.

One OK Rock

A Japanese rock band that blends rock, pop, and punk. Their emotional songs and powerful performances have made them popular with fans around the world.

SlipKnot

A heavy metal band known for intense performances, dark lyrics, and unique masks. Their music is loud, aggressive, and full of energy, attracting fans of hard rock and metal.

My Favorite Anime

Fireworks

A romantic fantasy about a boy and girl exploring parallel realities while debating how fireworks appear from different angles, leading to a journey of love and possibilities.
